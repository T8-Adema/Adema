ABOUT

STORY RICH BRAND DESIGN

ADEMA is a fragrance destination for those who appreciate iconic designer perfumes. We curate a selection of timeless scents from globally recognized fashion houses such as CK, Gucci, Dior, Chanel and Dolce & Gabbana.

At ADEMA, fragrance is more than a product - it is a personal signature and a way to express identity. Our mission is to make authentic luxury fragrances more accessible while celebrating the emotional connection people have with scent.

THE CORE AUDIENCE

ADEMA is designed for fragrance lovers who appreciate iconic designer scents. Our core audience is mainly women aged 16-35 who see perfume as part of their personal style and identity.

They are drawn to recognizable luxury fragrances that feel both timeless and modern, using scent as a way to express themselves.
